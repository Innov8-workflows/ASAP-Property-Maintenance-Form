Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

This policy explains how ASAP Property Maintenance Ltd ("we", "us", "our") collects and uses your personal information when you use this website or request a roof inspection or quote, and the rights you have under UK data protection law.

Who we are

ASAP Property Maintenance Ltd is a roofing and property maintenance business covering the West Midlands, Staffordshire and the surrounding areas. We are the "data controller" responsible for your personal information.

You can contact us about your data, or to make any of the requests below, by phone or WhatsApp on 07865 989617.

The information we collect

Information you give us — when you complete the quote form or book an inspection, we collect the details you provide, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (optional)
  • Your postcode / the property location
  • The type of work you need and how soon you need it
  • If you book a visit, the appointment time you choose (handled by our booking provider, Calendly)

Information collected automatically (only with your consent) — if you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics and the Meta (Facebook) Pixel to understand how people find and use the site and how our advertising performs. This may include your approximate location, IP address, device and browser type, the pages you view, and how you arrived at the site.

How and why we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry — to arrange your free inspection, prepare your quote and contact you about it. Our lawful basis is taking steps at your request before entering into a contract, and our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries.
  • To provide our services — to carry out the work and keep records of it. Our lawful basis is performance of our contract with you and compliance with our legal obligations.
  • To measure and improve the site and our advertising — using analytics and advertising cookies. Our lawful basis is your consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies, grouped as:

  • Essential — needed for the site to work and to remember your cookie choice. These are always on.
  • Analytics — Google Analytics (GA4), to measure visits and how the site is used.
  • Advertising — the Meta (Facebook) Pixel, to measure the performance of our adverts.

Analytics and advertising cookies are not loaded until you accept them on our cookie banner. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time using the Cookie settings link in the footer.

Who we share your information with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted providers who help us run our business and act on our instructions, including:

  • Calendly — to manage inspection bookings
  • Google — for website analytics
  • Meta Platforms — for advertising measurement

Some of these providers may process data outside the UK. Where they do, appropriate safeguards (such as UK-approved contract terms or adequacy decisions) are in place to protect your information. We may also disclose information where required by law.

How long we keep your information

We keep your information only for as long as we need it to deal with your enquiry and provide our services, and then for a reasonable period afterwards for our business records and to meet legal or tax obligations (typically up to six years). After that it is securely deleted.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct information that is wrong or incomplete
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Ask us to restrict or object to how we use it
  • Ask for a copy of certain information in a portable format
  • Withdraw your consent at any time (for anything based on consent)

To exercise any of these, please contact us on 07865 989617. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you are unhappy with how we have handled your information.

Keeping your information secure

We take appropriate measures to keep your information safe and to prevent unauthorised access to or loss of it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
